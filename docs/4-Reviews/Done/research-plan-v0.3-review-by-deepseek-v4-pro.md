# Review Report: Research Plan v0.3

**Document under review:** `docs/plan/research-plan-v0.3.md`  
**Reviewer:** deepseek-v4-pro  
**Date:** 2026-07-01  
**Review type:** Document review — feasibility, scientific defensibility, applicant-fit, and execution readiness  

---

## Summary Assessment

v0.3 is a substantial improvement over the earlier scope. The framing as a **feature-availability study** with a strict claim boundary, decision gates, and a single primary task (JetClass top-vs-QCD) is scientifically sound and well-matched to an undergraduate research portfolio. The core 36-run matrix and the hierarchy of axes (feature > data scale > architecture) are sensible.

**The two most consequential gaps** are: (1) the pretrained PET-style checkpoint — the central measurement instrument — is never named or sourced, which leaves the plan's feasibility unverifiable; and (2) Feature Config D remains undefined, which makes E0's exit criterion circular because D's validity cannot be audited until D's columns are chosen.

With those resolved, the plan is execution-ready. Without them, it is a design document with a missing specification at its core.
